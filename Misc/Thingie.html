Business Model Canvas: AI-Integrated Lip Tracking Web Application

1. Key Partners

  • Medical/Speech Professionals, Patient Support Groups
  • Open-source communities (TensorFlow, MediaPipe)

2. Key Activities

  • AI Model Development & Refinement (Lip Reading)
  • Web App Development & Maintenance (Health Tracking, Phrase Library)
  • Data Collection & Preprocessing

3. Value Proposition

  • Improves communication for speech-impaired (Laryngeal Cancer patients)
  • Predicts Arabic words from lip movements
  • Provides psychological benefit & health tracking; Arabic language support

4. Customer Relationships

  • Personal Assistance, Community, Self-Service, Automated Reminders

5. Customer Segments

  • Laryngeal Cancer patients (primary), other speech-impaired individuals
  • Caregivers, Medical Institutions

6. Key Resources

  • AI Model (CNN-RNN), Arabic Lip-Reading Dataset
  • AI/Web Development Talent, Cloud Infrastructure

7. Channels

  • Web Application (primary)
  • Medical Referrals, Patient Advocacy Groups

8. Cost Structure

  • R&D (AI model, features)
  • Infrastructure (servers, cloud)
  • Personnel (developers, researchers)
  • Data Acquisition

9. Revenue Streams (Future Considerations)

  • Subscription/Freemium Models
  • Partnerships with Healthcare Providers
  • Grants/Donations
